The Niagara River flows at approximately 35 miles/hour (56.3 kilometers/hour)..
The river is also used for fishing, irrigation, and bathing, and it is worshiped in the Hindu religion as the Mother Ganga.
The Yamuna River is approximately 1,376 kilometers (855 miles) long. It originates from the Yamunotri glacier in the state of Uttarakhand and flows through various states in northern India before merging with the Ganges River in Allahabad, Uttar Pradesh..
Dalhousie is a hill station, near town of Chamba in Chamba district in the Indian state of Himachal Pradesh. It is situated on five hills and has an elevation of 1,970 m (6,460 ft) above sea level.
