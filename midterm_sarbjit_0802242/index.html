Authentic Indian food

Dal Makhani is a popular North Indian dish made from black lentils (urad dal), red kidney beans (rajma), butter, and cream. It is typically cooked slowly, allowing the flavors to meld together, resulting in a rich and creamy texture.

Kadai Paneer is a popular Indian dish that originated in the Punjab region. It is made from paneer (Indian cottage cheese) cooked with a mix of bell peppers, onions, tomatoes, and aromatic spices.

Naan bread is a traditional Indian flatbread that has gained popularity worldwide for its soft, fluffy texture and delicious flavor.

Veg Biryani is a fragrant and flavorful rice dish that originates from the Indian subcontinent. It is a vegetarian variation of the traditional biryani, which is typically made with meat such as chicken, lamb, or goat. Veg Biryani is prepared by layering long-grain basmati rice with a medley of mixed vegetables, aromatic spices, and herbs.

Paneer pakora is a delicious Indian snack made from paneer (Indian cottage cheese) that is coated in a spiced chickpea flour batter and deep-fried until crispy.

Pani Puri, also known as Golgappa, Puchka, or Phuchka in different regions of India, is a popular street food snack enjoyed across the country. It consists of small, hollow, crispy puris (deep-fried dough balls) filled with a mixture of tangy, spicy, and savory ingredients.
